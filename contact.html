Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package on our partner's website.

What's your return policy?

We work with various retailers, each with their own return policy. Please check the specific retailer's policy for the product you're interested in.

How do I become a partner?

We're always looking for quality brands to partner with. Please use the contact form above and select "Partnership" as the category.

Can I write for your blog?

We welcome guest contributions! Send us your ideas through the contact form with some writing samples.

How are products selected?

Our team carefully curates products based on quality, design, functionality, and value. We also consider customer feedback and trends.

Do you offer design consultations?

While we don't offer personal consultations, our blog is full of design tips and advice. Feel free to ask specific questions through our contact form.
