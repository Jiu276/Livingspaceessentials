Our Story

Founded in 2020, Livingspaceessentials began as a passion project by a group of home decor enthusiasts who believed that everyone deserves to live in a beautiful, functional space. What started as a small blog sharing design tips has grown into a comprehensive platform offering carefully curated products, expert advice, and inspiration for modern living.
